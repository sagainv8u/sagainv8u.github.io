Our Story

  • First We Meet

    1995

    Friends, childhood friends. You read it right. B4 we even know wat s friendship, we got together as friends. It all started in 1995 as class mates. To know her existence in class, it took me 3 full years. We started talking to each other only in 1999. It was when she temporarily shifted her home near my home. We together commuted to school which is of more than a km by walk. We were sitting next to each other in class. We both were class toppers. We shared so much memory in class, in commute, in play As any Normal movie, there comes a twist of school change. Though we both were put up in same school, it was full boy, full girl classes. We never had a chance to talk. We both had our own friends, time, study to go on till 2015

  • School Days

    2005

    In 2005, again we both got to b in same class for next 2 years. We went tuition together. We had a gang of friends to roam along. Obviously mix of girls and boys. Study together ended here in 2007 just with schooling. We both had different dreams and followed it for next few years. Now comes digital revolution, which helped us to b and keep connected. For next few years, we were having regular chats, some calls, rare meets as friends

  • In A Relationship

    2013

    In 2013, her sister Marriage had put one more break in our contacts. Though it was short and did not long for days together, this time we could feel the difference. This is when we thought it has progressed to next stage. But being person with so much responsibilities in family, we both did not even talk abt it with each other. We sensed the difference. Our close friends sensed the difference. But we were silent to not spoil any family aspirations on both of us. It was God who made us to stick together for quite a long years. Let God decide how it should go

  • My Career

    2016

    In 2016, she was still not married, I came to a good state in my professional career. One of her marriage proposal made us to trigger our hidden feelings, we conveyed to each other. God said, Don't be so happy. I'm not ending this story here. Next comes my big family responsibilities, her family serious groom seeking story. Even this time, we decided to go with what God has in his plan. We didn't make much noise in family. Years passed. My responsibilities are settled.

  • Our life journey together

    2020

    Now sitting in 2020, the real struggle started as her family never accepted the proposal. This time we were stronger together and not leave this here as God has kept us together these many years to make us together for rest of our life. We also were so clear that until parents accept, we won't marry. Next one year was a quite a big struggle. Mentally, physically, emotionally. Both had worst experience. But never came down from our stand Parents got convinced at the end. May be God thought, enough of testing, Now we are here, inviting you for that big ceremony. We are going to start our life journey together. Nothing big changed though, just that we will be in same home. Our thoughts joined a long back.
